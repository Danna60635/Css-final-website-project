Real Madrid

Real Madrid is 123 years old, it was founded on march 6, 1902, as Madrid Soccer Club. Real Madrid has had Cristiano Ronaldo and Karim Benzema as their top scorers. Cristiano with 450 goals and Benzema with 323 goals. Real Madrid has won over 100 official trophies, a record of 15 Champions League, a record 36 La Liga titles, and 20 Copa del Rey trophies.
